Resume

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the department of Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University working with Prof. Xin Li on error correcting codes. I completed my Ph.D from Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Elena Grigorescu .

Research

I am interested in well-motivated algorithmic problems with some algebraic flavor. My research so far has focused on the near neighbor search problem and its variants on unstructured data and on structured algebraic objects such as lattices and error-correcting codes.
